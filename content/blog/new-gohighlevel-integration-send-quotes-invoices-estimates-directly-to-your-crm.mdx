> We’re excited to announce our newest integration: **GoHighLevel**.
> 
> If your practice uses GoHighLevel (or a CRM built on it like AesthetixCRM) you can now connect it to MySurgeryQuote and streamline your entire quoting workflow.
> 
> **Here’s what you can do:**
> 
> -   **Send quote PDFs** to any GHL contact with one click
> -   **Generate invoices** from your quote line items, no re-entering data
> -   **Create patient-facing estimates** that your patient receives by email with an accept or decline option, with a 30-day expiry
> -   **Search contacts** by name, email, or phone directly from MySurgeryQuote
> -   **Automatic tagging:** every time you send a quote, invoice, or estimate, the contact is tagged (`quote-sent`, `invoice-sent`, `estimate-sent`) so you can trigger automations in GHL
> 
> **Setup takes under a minute.** Go to Settings, click Connect next to GoHighLevel, authorize through the GHL Marketplace, and you’re done. No API keys, no technical setup.
> 
> **Included on every plan** at no extra cost, just like our ModMed and DrChrono integrations.
> 
> This is a big one for practices that rely on GoHighLevel for patient communication and follow-up. Your quoting and CRM workflows are now fully connected.
