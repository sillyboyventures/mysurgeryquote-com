## The Mommy Makeover quote test

Take a realistic case. A patient wants a Mommy Makeover with Dr. Koehler: breast augmentation ($5,500), tummy tuck ($7,500), and liposuction ($3,500). OR time is 4.5 hours. Two implants. It's an ordinary cosmetic quote, and it's a good stress test.

**In Nextech's estimate.** You list the procedure charges. Then you work out the facility fee by hand from the 4.5-hour OR time, calculate anesthesia the same way, and enter the implant costs as separate lines. The estimate captures the numbers you supply, but it doesn't run the cosmetic fee formulas for you, and it doesn't credit the coordinator who closed the case.

**In MySurgeryQuote.** Select Dr. Koehler from the dropdown. His pricing loads automatically. Add the three procedures. The facility and anesthesia fees calculate themselves from the 4.5-hour time. Pick the two implants from the catalog. Generate the quote. Realistic time: about 90 seconds.

The difference isn't that Nextech can't produce an estimate. It's that Nextech treats each fee as a number you supply, while a purpose-built tool treats fees as formulas it runs for you, and shows its math.
