Include retail products like skincare, serums, and post-treatment kits directly on patient quotes.

**Setting Up Products**

1.  Go to **Admin → Products**
2.  Click **Add Product**
3.  Enter:
    -   **Product Name** (e.g., “ZO Skin Health Daily Power Defense”)
    -   **SKU** (optional)
    -   **Price**
    -   **Category** (optional – for organization)
4.  Save the product

**Adding Products to Quotes**

1.  Create a new quote
2.  On the appropriate step, scroll to **Products**
3.  Select products to add
4.  Adjust quantity if needed
5.  Products appear as line items on the quote

**On the Patient Quote**

Products appear in their own section with name, quantity, and price.
