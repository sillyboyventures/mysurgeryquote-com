### Accessing Practice Settings

1.  Click **Admin** in the main navigation
2.  Select **Settings** from the dropdown

### What You Can Configure

**Practice Information**

-   Practice Name
-   Logo (upload your practice logo for PDFs and emails)
-   Phone, Email, Website
-   Address

**Brand Colors**

-   Primary Color – Used in PDF headers and email templates
-   Accent Color – Used for highlights and buttons

**Quote Settings**

-   Default Quote Disclaimer – Text that appears at the bottom of every quote PDF (cancellation policies, payment terms, etc.)

**Email Template**

-   Email Subject Line
-   Introduction Paragraph
-   Closing Message

### Saving Changes

After making any changes, click **Save Settings** at the bottom of the page. Your changes will apply to all new quotes immediately.
