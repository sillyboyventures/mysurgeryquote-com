Many med spas charge different rates depending on who performs the treatment. An MD might charge more than an RN for the same service. Provider tiers let you set pricing by credential level.

**Setting Up Provider Tiers**

1.  Go to **Admin → Provider Tiers**
2.  Click **Add Tier**
3.  Enter:
    -   **Tier Name** (e.g., “RN”, “NP”, “PA”, “MD”)
    -   **Price Adjustment** – percentage or dollar amount modifier
4.  Save the tier

**Assigning Tiers to Providers**

1.  Go to **Admin → Providers**
2.  Edit a provider
3.  Select their **Provider Tier**
4.  Save

**How It Works in Quotes**

When you select a provider during quote creation, their tier’s pricing adjustment automatically applies to applicable procedures.

**Example**

-   Base Botox price: $13/unit
-   RN tier: Base price
-   MD tier: +20%
-   Patient getting Botox from MD: $15.60/unit
