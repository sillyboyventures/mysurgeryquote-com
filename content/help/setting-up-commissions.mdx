> Configure commission rates for staff members under Admin > Commissions. Create commission groups (e.g., “Patient Care Coordinators”) or set individual rates. Rates can vary by provider – for example, 5% for Dr. Smith’s cases, 3% for Dr. Jones. Only invoiced quotes count toward commissions.
