Med spa quotes follow a simplified workflow compared to surgical quotes.

**Step-by-Step Guide**

**Step 1: Patient & Provider**

-   Enter patient name
-   Enter patient email (optional – for emailing the quote)
-   Select a provider (optional – can assign later)
-   Select membership tier if applicable

**Step 2: Treatments**

-   Search or browse treatments by category
-   Click to add treatments to the quote
-   For injectables, enter the unit quantity
-   Add notes if needed (e.g., treatment areas)

**Step 3: Discounts**

-   Apply per-treatment discounts if needed
-   Review membership discount if applied
-   Add discount reason (for internal tracking)

**Step 4: Review & Save**

-   Review all line items and totals
-   Add internal notes (not shown to patient)
-   Click **Save Quote**

**After Saving**

-   Download PDF
-   Email to patient
-   Quote is saved to Quote History
