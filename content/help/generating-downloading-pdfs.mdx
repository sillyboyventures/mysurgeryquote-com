### Creating a Quote PDF

1.  Open any saved quote from the Quotes list
2.  Click **Download PDF**
3.  The PDF will download to your device

### What’s Included in the PDF

-   Your practice logo and branding
-   Patient name and quote date
-   Detailed breakdown of:
    -   Procedures with codes and pricing
    -   Implants with quantities, codes, and any discounts
    -   Additional fees
-   Total quote amount
-   Your practice’s disclaimer text

### PDF Branding

Your PDF automatically uses your practice branding configured in Settings:

-   Practice logo (top of PDF)
-   Practice name and contact information
-   Primary brand color in the header
-   Custom disclaimer text at the bottom
