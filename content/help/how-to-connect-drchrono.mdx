This guide walks you through connecting your DrChrono account to MySurgeryQuote using secure OAuth2 authorization.

### Before You Begin

You’ll need:

-   Admin access to your DrChrono account
-   Permission to create API applications in DrChrono

### Step 1: Create a DrChrono API Application

1.  Log into your **DrChrono account**
2.  Click your profile icon → **Account Settings**
3.  Navigate to **API** in the left sidebar
4.  Click **Create New Application** (or use an existing one)
5.  Configure the application:
    -   **Name:** MySurgeryQuote
    -   **Redirect URI:** Copy this from MySurgeryQuote’s EMR Integration page
6.  Save the application
7.  Copy your **Client ID** and **Client Secret**

### Step 2: Enter Credentials in MySurgeryQuote

1.  Go to **Admin → EMR Integration**
2.  In the DrChrono section, enter:
    -   **Client ID** – From your DrChrono API application
    -   **Client Secret** – From your DrChrono API application
3.  Click **Save Credentials**

### Step 3: Authorize the Connection

1.  Click **Connect to DrChrono**
2.  You’ll be redirected to DrChrono’s login page
3.  Log in with your DrChrono credentials
4.  Review the permissions and click **Authorize**
5.  You’ll be redirected back to MySurgeryQuote

### Step 4: Verify the Connection

1.  You should see a green “Connected” status
2.  Click **Test Connection** to verify everything is working

### Sending Quotes to DrChrono

Once connected:

1.  Open any quote
2.  Click **Send to DrChrono**
3.  Search for the patient by name
4.  Select the patient from the results
5.  Click **Send** to upload the quote PDF to the patient’s documents

### Disconnecting DrChrono

If you need to disconnect or reconnect:

1.  Go to **Admin → EMR Integration**
2.  Click **Disconnect** in the DrChrono section
3.  Your credentials will be cleared
4.  Follow the setup steps again to reconnect
