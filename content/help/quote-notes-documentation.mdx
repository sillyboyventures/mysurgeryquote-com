### Adding Notes to Quotes

Notes help document important details that don’t appear on the patient PDF.

### Types of Notes

**Internal Notes** (Staff Only)

-   Discount reasons
-   Special circumstances
-   Follow-up reminders
-   Conversation details

**Patient-Visible Notes**

-   Special instructions
-   Preparation requirements
-   Important disclaimers specific to this quote

### Adding Notes During Quote Creation

In the Adjustments step of the quote wizard:

1.  Find the Notes section
2.  Enter your notes
3.  Select visibility (internal or patient-visible)
4.  Continue with the quote

### Editing Notes Later

1.  Open the quote
2.  Click **Edit Quote**
3.  Navigate to the Adjustments step
4.  Update notes as needed
5.  Save the quote

### Best Practices

-   Document why discounts were applied
-   Note any verbal agreements with patients
-   Record consultation details
-   Add follow-up reminders
