Quick Quotes are pre-built templates for common procedure combinations. Instead of building the same quote from scratch, apply a template with one click.

**Creating a Quick Quote Template**

1.  Go to **Admin → Quick Quotes**
2.  Click **Add Template**
3.  Enter a **Template Name** (e.g., “Mommy Makeover”, “Upper Bleph + Brow Lift”)
4.  Add procedures to the template
5.  Optionally set default discounts or notes
6.  Save the template

**Using Quick Quotes**

1.  Start a new quote
2.  On the Procedures step, click **Apply Template**
3.  Select your Quick Quote template
4.  All procedures from the template are added to the quote
5.  Modify as needed for the specific patient

**Best Practices**

-   Create templates for your most common combinations
-   Name templates clearly
-   Review and update templates periodically as pricing changes
