If your practice offers both surgical procedures and med spa treatments, MySurgeryQuote handles both from a single account.

**How It Works**

When your practice type is set to “Both”:

-   You can create **Surgical Quotes** with facility fees, anesthesia, and implants
-   You can create **Med Spa Quotes** with unit pricing and membership discounts
-   Both quote types share the same patient history and dashboard

**Choosing Quote Type**

When you click **New Quote**, you’ll select the quote type:

-   **Surgical** – Full surgical workflow
-   **Med Spa** – Streamlined med spa workflow

The system automatically shows only relevant options based on your selection.

**Staff Setup**

Your staff can be configured as:

-   **Surgeons** – Appear in surgical quotes
-   **Providers** – Appear in med spa quotes

Go to **Admin → Surgeons/Providers** and set the **Provider Type** for each staff member.
