### I can’t log in

-   Double-check your email address for typos
-   Use the “Forgot Password?” link to reset your password
-   Make sure Caps Lock is not on
-   Contact your administrator to verify your account is active

### My changes didn’t save

-   Make sure you clicked the **Save** button
-   Check for any error messages at the top of the page
-   Try refreshing the page and making changes again
-   If the issue persists, contact us at [https://www.mysurgeryquote.com/contact/](https://www.mysurgeryquote.com/contact/)

### Procedures are missing when creating a quote

-   Procedures must be assigned to the selected surgeon
-   Go to **Admin > Procedures** and check the surgeon assignments
-   Make sure the procedure is set to Active

### PDF doesn’t show my logo

-   Go to **Admin > Settings** and upload your logo
-   Logos should be PNG or JPG format
-   Recommended size: 300×100 pixels or similar aspect ratio
-   Click **Save Settings** after uploading

### Patient didn’t receive the email

-   Verify the patient’s email address is correct
-   Ask the patient to check their spam/junk folder
-   Some email providers may delay delivery
-   If emails consistently fail, contact us at [https://www.mysurgeryquote.com/contact/](https://www.mysurgeryquote.com/contact/)
