Membership tiers let you offer automatic percentage discounts to VIP patients or members of your loyalty program.

**Setting Up Membership Tiers**

1.  Go to **Admin → Membership Tiers**
2.  Click **Add Tier**
3.  Enter:
    -   **Tier Name** (e.g., “Gold Member”, “VIP”, “Platinum”)
    -   **Discount Percentage** (e.g., 15%)
    -   **Description** (optional, for internal reference)
4.  Save the tier

**Example Tiers**

| Tier | Discount |
| --- | --- |
| Silver | 10% |
| Gold | 15% |
| Platinum | 20% |

**Applying Membership Discounts to Quotes**

1.  Start a new med spa quote
2.  On the Patient & Provider step, select the patient’s **Membership Tier**
3.  Continue building the quote as normal
4.  The membership discount automatically applies to all treatment fees
5.  The discount appears as a line item on the quote summary

**On the Patient Quote**

The PDF shows the membership tier name and the total discount amount applied.

### Article 4: Treatment Series

**Title:** Treatment Series (Packages)

**Content:**

Treatment series let you sell prepaid packages like “Buy 5 HydraFacials, Get 1 Free” with built-in savings for patients.

**Setting Up Treatment Series**

1.  Go to **Admin → Treatment Series**
2.  Click **Add Series**
3.  Enter:
    -   **Series Name** (e.g., “HydraFacial Package of 6”)
    -   **Number of Treatments** (e.g., 6)
    -   **Total Price** (e.g., $995 – representing 5 paid + 1 free)
    -   **Linked Procedure** (optional – connects to a specific treatment)
4.  Save the series

**Example Series**

| Series Name | Treatments | Price | Savings |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Botox Club – 3 Sessions | 3 | $750 | $150 |
| HydraFacial 6-Pack | 6 | $995 | $199 |
| Laser Hair 8-Session | 8 | $1,600 | $400 |

**Adding Series to Quotes**

1.  Create a new med spa quote
2.  On the Procedures step, scroll to **Treatment Series**
3.  Select the series and quantity
4.  The package price is added to the quote

**On the Patient Quote**

The PDF shows the series name, number of treatments included, and total package price.
