### Before You Can Email

Make sure the patient has an email address saved on their quote. You can add or edit this from the quote detail page.

### Sending a Quote Email

1.  Open the quote you want to send
2.  Click **Email Quote**
3.  Review the preview (optional)
4.  Click **Send**

### What the Patient Receives

The patient will receive an email containing:

-   Your practice branding
-   A personalized message (configured in your Email Template settings)
-   The quote PDF as an attachment

### Customizing Email Templates

Go to **Admin > Settings** to customize:

-   Email subject line
-   Introduction paragraph
-   Closing message

### Tracking Sent Quotes

After emailing, the quote status changes to “Sent” and the date/time is recorded in the quote history.
