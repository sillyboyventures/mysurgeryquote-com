When your practice has multiple surgical facilities, you’ll be asked to select which facility the procedure will be performed at when creating a quote.

**Selecting a Facility**

1.  Begin creating a new quote
2.  In the **Time & Fees** step, you’ll see a facility selector
3.  Choose the facility where the surgery will take place
4.  The facility name will appear on the quote and PDF

**First-Time Setup**

The first time you create a quote after adding multiple facilities, you’ll be prompted to select a facility. Your selection will be remembered for future quotes, but you can change it at any time.

**Why Facility Selection Matters**

-   **Quotes and PDFs** display the facility name for patient clarity
-   **EMR billing** uses the facility’s EMR Location ID to send charges to the correct location in your EMR system
-   **Fee calculations** are based on the fee settings configured for your practice
