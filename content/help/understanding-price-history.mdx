### What is Price History?

MySurgeryQuote automatically tracks every price change for:

-   Procedures
-   Implants
-   Additional fees

This creates an audit trail showing what prices were in effect at any time.

### Why Price History Matters

-   **Audit Compliance** – Document when and why prices changed
-   **Quote Accuracy** – Quotes use the price at time of creation
-   **Reporting** – Analyze pricing trends over time
-   **Dispute Resolution** – Verify what price was quoted

### Viewing Price History

1.  Go to **Admin > Procedures** (or Implants/Fees)
2.  Click on any item
3.  Look for the **Price History** section
4.  View all historical prices with effective dates

### What’s Tracked

For each price change:

-   Previous price
-   New price
-   Date of change
-   User who made the change

### Quotes and Historical Prices

When you create a quote:

-   The current price is captured
-   The quote retains that price even if you later change the procedure price
-   This ensures quote accuracy and integrity
