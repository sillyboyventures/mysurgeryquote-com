### Editing an Existing Quote

1.  Go to **Quotes** and find the quote you want to edit
2.  Click on the quote to open it
3.  Click **Edit Quote**
4.  Make your changes through the quote wizard
5.  Click **Save** when done

### What Can Be Edited

-   Patient information
-   Surgeon selection
-   Procedures (add, remove, adjust pricing)
-   Implants (add, remove, adjust pricing/quantity)
-   Additional fees
-   Discounts
-   Notes

### When to Edit vs. Create New

**Edit the existing quote when:**

-   Fixing a typo
-   Minor price adjustment
-   Adding a forgotten item

**Create a new quote when:**

-   Significant changes to procedures
-   Different surgical plan
-   Need to preserve the original for comparison

### Duplicating a Quote

To create a similar quote for a new patient:

1.  Open the original quote
2.  Click **Duplicate Quote**
3.  Update the patient information
4.  Adjust procedures/pricing as needed
5.  Save the new quote

This is useful for:

-   Common procedure combinations
-   Similar surgical plans
-   Template-style quotes

### Quote Version History

All changes to quotes are tracked. You can view the history to see:

-   When changes were made
-   What was modified
-   Who made the changes
