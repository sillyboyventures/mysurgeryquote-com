Once your GoHighLevel integration is connected, you can send quote PDFs and create invoices directly from any quote.

**Sending a Quote PDF**

1.  Open a quote from your quote list
2.  Click **Send to GoHighLevel**
3.  Search for the patient by name, email, or phone number
4.  Select the matching GoHighLevel contact
5.  Choose **Send Quote PDF**
6.  Click **Send**

The quote PDF will be uploaded to the contact’s records in GoHighLevel.

**Creating an Invoice**

1.  Open a quote from your quote list
2.  Click **Send to GoHighLevel**
3.  Search for and select the GoHighLevel contact
4.  Choose **Send Invoice**
5.  Click **Send**

An invoice will be created in GoHighLevel with all the line items from the quote, including surgeon fees, facility fees, anesthesia fees, and any additional fees.

**Tips**

-   Make sure the patient exists as a contact in GoHighLevel before sending
-   Invoices include all line items at the amounts shown on the quote
-   You can send both a PDF and an invoice for the same quote
