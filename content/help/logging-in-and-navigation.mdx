## Accessing MySurgeryQuote

Your practice has a dedicated URL for MySurgeryQuote. Bookmark this link for easy access.

## Logging In

1.  Go to your practice’s MySurgeryQuote URL
2.  Enter your email address
3.  Enter your password
4.  Click “Sign In”

## Forgot Your Password?

1.  Click “Forgot Password?” on the login page
2.  Enter your email address
3.  Check your email for a reset link
4.  Click the link and create a new password

## Main Navigation

After logging in, you’ll see the main menu:

-   **Dashboard** – Overview of recent quotes
-   **New Quote** – Create a new patient quote
-   **Quotes** – View and search all quotes
-   **Admin** – Settings, procedures, users (admin only)
