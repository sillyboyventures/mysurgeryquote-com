Existing surgical practices can add med spa quoting capabilities for $50/month per location.

**How to Add Med Spa**

1.  Contact support or visit your billing portal
2.  Select the “Add Med Spa” option
3.  Med spa features will be enabled within 24 hours

**What You Get**

-   Med spa quote workflow
-   Unit-based pricing for injectables
-   Membership tiers
-   Treatment series
-   Provider management
-   Med spa procedure categories

**Your Existing Setup**

All your existing surgical procedures, surgeons, and settings remain unchanged. Med spa is added alongside your surgical capabilities.

**Pricing**

-   $50/month per location
-   Added to your existing subscription
-   No long-term contract required
