### Viewing All Quotes

Click **Quotes** in the main navigation to see all quotes for your practice.

### Filtering Quotes

Use the filters at the top to narrow down your list:

-   **Status** – Draft, Sent, Accepted, Expired
-   **Date Range** – Filter by creation date
-   **Search** – Find quotes by patient name or quote number

### Quote Statuses

-   **Draft** – Quote has been created but not yet sent to patient
-   **Sent** – Quote has been emailed to the patient
-   **Accepted** – Patient has accepted the quote
-   **Expired** – Quote has passed its validity period

### Viewing Quote Details

Click on any quote to view its full details, including:

-   Patient information
-   All procedures, implants, and fees
-   Total amounts and any discounts applied
-   Quote history and status changes

### Downloading PDFs

From the quote detail page, click **Download PDF** to generate a professional PDF quote document.

### Emailing Quotes

Click **Email Quote** to send the quote directly to the patient’s email address. The email will include:

-   Your practice branding
-   A personalized message
-   The quote PDF as an attachment
