MySurgeryQuote is a quote builder designed specifically for plastic surgery practices. It helps you create professional, accurate patient quotes in minutes.

## What You Can Do

-   **Create Quotes** – Build detailed surgical quotes with procedures, implants, and fees
-   **Apply Discounts** – Add percentage or fixed discounts to any line item
-   **Generate PDFs** – Professional quotes with your practice branding
-   **Email Quotes** – Send quotes directly to patients
-   **Track History** – View and manage all past quotes
