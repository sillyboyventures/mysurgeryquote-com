### Starting a New Quote

1.  Click **New Quote** from the main navigation
2.  You’ll be guided through a step-by-step wizard

### Step 1: Patient Information

Enter the patient’s basic details:

-   First Name (required)
-   Last Name (required)
-   Email (optional – needed if you want to email the quote)
-   Phone (optional)

### Step 2: Select Surgeon

Choose the surgeon who will perform the procedure(s). The surgeon selection determines which procedures are available.

### Step 3: Select Procedures

-   Browse procedures by category or use the search box
-   Click a procedure to add it to your quote
-   Selected procedures appear in the blue highlighted box on the right
-   Click the X to remove a procedure

### Step 4: Implants (if applicable)

If your procedures require implants:

-   Select the implant brand
-   Choose the specific implant type
-   Enter quantity
-   Apply any discounts if applicable

### Step 5: Additional Fees

Add any additional fees such as:

-   Facility fees
-   Anesthesia fees
-   Lab work
-   Post-operative garments

### Step 6: Adjustments

Review the quote total and make any final adjustments:

-   Apply overall discounts
-   Add notes that will appear on the quote

### Step 7: Review & Save

-   Review all details
-   Click **Save Quote** to create the quote
-   You can then download the PDF or email it to the patient
