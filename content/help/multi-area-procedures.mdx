Some procedures like liposuction or laser treatments can be performed on multiple areas. Multi-area procedures let you specify a quantity while keeping it as one line item.

**Enabling Multi-Area for a Procedure**

1.  Go to **Admin → Procedures**
2.  Edit the procedure
3.  Check **“Can Have Additional Areas”**
4.  Save

**Using Multi-Area in Quotes**

1.  Add a multi-area procedure to a quote
2.  A quantity selector appears
3.  Enter the number of areas (e.g., 3)
4.  Use the notes field to specify which areas
5.  Price multiplies by quantity

**Example**

Laser Hair Removal – $250/area

-   Quantity: 3
-   Notes: “Underarms, Bikini, Lower Legs”
-   Total: $750
