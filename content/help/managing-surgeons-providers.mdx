Practices offering both surgical and med spa services need to distinguish between surgeons (who perform surgery) and providers (who perform med spa treatments).

**Provider Types**

-   **Surgeon** – Appears in surgical quote workflow
-   **Provider** – Appears in med spa quote workflow

Staff members can only be one type. If someone does both, create them as a Surgeon (they can still be referenced in med spa contexts if needed).

**Setting Provider Type**

1.  Go to **Admin → Surgeons** (or **Providers** depending on your practice type)
2.  Click **Add** or edit an existing staff member
3.  Select **Provider Type**:
    -   Surgeon
    -   Provider
4.  Save

**In the Quote Builder**

-   Surgical quotes show only staff marked as “Surgeon”
-   Med spa quotes show only staff marked as “Provider”
