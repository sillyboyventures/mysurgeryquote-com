Once your EMR is connected, you can send quotes directly to patient charts with a few clicks.

### Sending a Quote

1.  Open the quote you want to send
2.  Click the **Send to \[EMR Name\]** button
3.  Search for the patient by name
4.  Select the correct patient from the results
5.  Click **Send**

### What Gets Sent

**To Patient Documents:**

-   A PDF copy of the quote is uploaded to the patient’s chart
-   The document is labeled with the quote number and date

**Billing Charges (ModMed only):**

-   Optionally create billing line items for each procedure
-   Charges appear in ModMed ready for processing

### Tracking Sent Quotes

After sending:

-   The quote shows “Sent to \[EMR\]” with timestamp
-   You can see who sent it and when
-   Sent quotes cannot be deleted by regular users (admin only)

### Resending a Quote

If you need to send an updated quote:

1.  Make your changes to the quote
2.  Save the quote
3.  Click **Send to \[EMR Name\]** again
4.  A new document will be uploaded (previous version remains)

### Tips

-   Always verify you’re selecting the correct patient before sending
-   For patients with common names, check their date of birth in the EMR
-   Send quotes promptly after creation for accurate record-keeping
