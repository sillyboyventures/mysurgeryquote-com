Apply practice-wide price increases or decreases to all procedures at once.

**Creating an Adjustment**

1.  Go to **Admin → Fee Settings**
2.  Scroll to **Global Adjustments**
3.  Click **Add Adjustment**
4.  Choose:
    -   **Increase** or **Decrease**
    -   **Percentage** or **Dollar Amount**
5.  Enter the value (e.g., 5%)
6.  Optionally exclude specific categories
7.  Apply the adjustment

**Excluding Categories**

Some procedures shouldn’t be affected by global adjustments. When creating an adjustment:

1.  Click **Exclude Categories**
2.  Check categories to exclude (e.g., “Non-Surgical” or “Injectables”)
3.  Those procedures won’t be affected

**Adjustment History**

All adjustments are logged with date, amount, and who made the change. View history in Fee Settings.
