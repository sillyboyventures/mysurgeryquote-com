### What Each Practice Can Customize

Every practice on MySurgeryQuote has full control over their own branding and settings:

**Visual Branding**

-   Practice logo (appears on PDFs and emails)
-   Primary brand color
-   Accent color

**Practice Information**

-   Practice name
-   Address
-   Phone number
-   Website URL

**Quote Settings**

-   Default disclaimer text
-   Quote validity period

**Email Templates**

-   Subject line
-   Introduction message
-   Closing message

### Custom Domains

Some practices may have a custom domain instead of a subdomain. For example:

-   quotebuilder.yourpractice.com instead of yourpractice.mysurgeryquote.com

Custom domains are configured during onboarding. Contact us at [https://www.mysurgeryquote.com/contact/](https://www.mysurgeryquote.com/contact/) if you’re interested.

### Branding Best Practices

-   **Logo:** Use a high-resolution PNG with transparent background (recommended 300×100 pixels)
-   **Colors:** Choose colors that match your practice’s existing branding
-   **Disclaimer:** Include your cancellation policy, payment terms, and any legal requirements
