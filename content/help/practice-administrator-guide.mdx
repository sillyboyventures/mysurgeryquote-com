### Your Role as Practice Administrator

As a Practice Administrator, you have full control over your practice’s MySurgeryQuote setup. This guide covers your key responsibilities.

### Initial Setup Checklist

When your practice first joins MySurgeryQuote:

-    Upload your practice logo
-    Set your brand colors
-    Enter practice contact information
-    Configure your quote disclaimer
-    Add all surgeons
-    Import/create all procedures with pricing
-    Assign procedures to each surgeon
-    Set up additional fees (facility, anesthesia, etc.)
-    Add implant options if applicable
-    Create user accounts for staff
-    Test by creating a sample quote

### Ongoing Responsibilities

**Regular Maintenance**

-   Update procedure pricing as needed
-   Add new procedures when offered
-   Manage user accounts (add new staff, remove former employees)
-   Update practice information if it changes

**Quality Control**

-   Review quotes periodically for accuracy
-   Ensure pricing is current
-   Keep surgeon-procedure assignments up to date

### What You Control vs. Platform Level

**You Control:**

-   All practice settings and branding
-   Users within your practice
-   Procedures, pricing, and fees
-   Surgeons and their assignments
-   Quotes and patient data

**Platform Level (MySurgeryQuote manages):**

-   Your practice subdomain or custom domain
-   System updates and new features
-   Technical infrastructure
-   Platform security
