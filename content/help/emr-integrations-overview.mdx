*MySurgeryQuote integrates with popular Electronic Medical Record and CRM systems to streamline your workflow. Send patient quotes directly to their chart or CRM record without leaving the application.*

### Supported EMR Systems

**ModMed (Modernizing Medicine)**

-   Send quote PDFs to patient documents
-   Automatically create billing charges
-   Search patients directly from MySurgeryQuote

**DrChrono (by EverHealth)**

-   Send quote PDFs to patient documents
-   Search patients directly from MySurgeryQuote
-   Secure OAuth2 connection

**GoHighLevel**

-   Send quote PDFs to contacts
-   Create invoices from quote line items
-   Search contacts by name, email, or phone
-   Secure OAuth2 connection

**Nextech** *(Coming soon)*

-   Nextech integration is in development and will support the same one-click quote send

### Included on every plan

All EMR integrations (ModMed, DrChrono, and GoHighLevel) are included on every plan at no extra cost. Nextech will be included on every plan when it launches.

### Getting Started

1.  Navigate to **Admin → EMR Integration** in your MySurgeryQuote dashboard
2.  Select your EMR provider
3.  Follow the setup instructions for your system
4.  Test the connection before use

### Requirements

-   Admin or Organization Admin role required for setup
-   Active account with your EMR provider
-   API access enabled on your EMR account (your EMR provider can assist)
