### New Practice Onboarding

When a new practice joins MySurgeryQuote, the following is configured:

1.  **Practice subdomain** – Your unique URL (e.g., yourpractice.mysurgeryquote.com)
2.  **Admin account** – The first administrator account for your practice
3.  **Initial setup** – Basic practice information

### First Steps for New Practices

After receiving your login credentials:

1.  **Log in** to your practice URL
2.  **Update Practice Settings**
    -   Upload your logo
    -   Set your brand colors
    -   Enter practice contact information
    -   Configure your quote disclaimer
3.  **Add Surgeons** – Create profiles for each surgeon
4.  **Set Up Procedures** – Add your procedures with pricing
5.  **Assign Procedures to Surgeons** – Link procedures to each surgeon
6.  **Add Additional Fees** – Configure facility fees, anesthesia, etc.
7.  **Invite Users** – Add staff members who need access
