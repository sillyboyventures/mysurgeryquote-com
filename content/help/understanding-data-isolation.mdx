### Complete Data Separation

Each practice on MySurgeryQuote operates in complete isolation. This means:

**Your Data is Yours**

-   Only your practice can see your quotes
-   Only your practice can see your patient information
-   Only your practice can see your pricing

**Separate Everything**  
Each practice has their own:

-   Patient quotes and history
-   Procedures and pricing
-   Surgeons
-   Implant catalog
-   Additional fees
-   User accounts
-   Practice settings

### Security & Privacy

-   Users can only log in to their assigned practice
-   There is no way for one practice to access another practice’s data
-   All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
-   HIPAA-compliant data handling

### What This Means for You

-   Set your own pricing without competitors seeing it
-   Your patient information stays private to your practice
-   Customize your procedures and fees independently
-   Your staff only sees your practice’s data
