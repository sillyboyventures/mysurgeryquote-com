MySurgeryQuote supports medical spa practices with a streamlined quoting workflow designed specifically for non-surgical treatments.

**How Med Spa Quoting Differs from Surgical**

Med spa quotes are simpler than surgical quotes because they don’t include:

-   Facility fees
-   Anesthesia fees
-   Implant selection
-   Surgical time calculations

Instead, med spa quotes focus on:

-   Treatment pricing (flat fee or unit-based)
-   Provider selection (optional)
-   Membership discounts
-   Treatment packages

**Practice Types**

Your practice can be configured as one of three types:

-   **Surgical Only** – Traditional surgical quote workflow
-   **Med Spa Only** – Streamlined med spa workflow
-   **Both** – Access to both surgical and med spa quoting

Your practice type is set during onboarding or can be changed in Practice Settings.

**Key Med Spa Features**

-   Unit-based pricing for injectables (by units, syringes, or vials)
-   Membership tiers for VIP patient discounts
-   Treatment series for package deals
-   Provider tier pricing by credential
-   Products to add retail items to quotes
