Injectables like Botox and dermal fillers are typically priced per unit, syringe, or vial rather than a flat fee. MySurgeryQuote supports all three pricing models.

**Setting Up Unit-Based Pricing**

1.  Go to **Admin → Procedures**
2.  Click **Add Procedure** or edit an existing injectable
3.  In the **Unit Type** dropdown, select:
    -   **Units** – For neurotoxins (Botox, Dysport, Xeomin)
    -   **Syringes** – For most dermal fillers (Juvederm, Restylane)
    -   **Vials** – For products sold by vial (Sculptra, Kybella)
4.  Set the **Price** as the per-unit cost (e.g., $13 per unit for Botox)
5.  Save the procedure

**Using Unit-Based Pricing in Quotes**

When adding a unit-based procedure to a quote:

1.  Select the procedure from the list
2.  Enter the **quantity** (e.g., 50 units)
3.  The system automatically calculates: 50 units × $13 = $650
4.  Add optional notes (e.g., “Forehead and crow’s feet”)

**On the Patient Quote**

The PDF shows:

-   Procedure name
-   Quantity and unit type (e.g., “50 units”)
-   Calculated total price
-   Any notes you added
