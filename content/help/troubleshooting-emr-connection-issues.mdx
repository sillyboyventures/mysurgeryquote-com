Having trouble with your EMR integration? Here are solutions to common issues.

### ModMed Issues

**“Connection Failed” or “Invalid Credentials”**

-   Double-check your Firm URL Prefix, Username, Password, and API Key
-   Ensure there are no extra spaces before or after credentials
-   Confirm your API access is still active with ModMed

**“Patient Not Found”**

-   Try searching with fewer characters (e.g., last name only)
-   Verify the patient exists in ModMed
-   Check that the patient name spelling matches exactly

**“Failed to Send Quote”**

-   Ensure you selected a patient before sending
-   Check your internet connection
-   Try disconnecting and reconnecting

### DrChrono Issues

**“Authorization Failed”**

-   Verify your Client ID and Client Secret are correct
-   Make sure the Redirect URI in DrChrono matches exactly
-   Try creating a new API application in DrChrono

**“Connection Expired”**

-   DrChrono tokens expire periodically
-   Click **Disconnect** then **Connect to DrChrono** to reauthorize

**“Patient Not Found”**

-   Search using the patient’s name as it appears in DrChrono
-   Try searching by last name only
-   Verify the patient exists in your DrChrono account

**“Failed to Upload Document”**

-   Ensure your DrChrono account has document storage enabled
-   Check that you have permission to add patient documents
-   Try disconnecting and reconnecting the integration
